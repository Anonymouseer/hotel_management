Order Management

How to take a manual order?

On the right sidebar, select the order type (Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery). Enter the customer's name and table/room number. Add items from the product list in the main content area.

How to handle new orders from the Kiosk?

New orders from the customer kiosk will automatically appear in the "Order Queues" tab. They will have a "pending" status. Click on the order to view details.

Customer Assistance

What to do when a "Help Request" notification appears?

A notification will appear on the bottom-right of the screen indicating which customer or room needs help. Please attend to them immediately.

System Management

How to manage products and inventory?

Go to the "Management" tab in the POS. From there, you can access "Menu Management" to add or edit products, and "Inventory" to update stock levels.

How to view sales reports?

In the "Management" tab, click on "Sales Reports". You can select a date range and generate a report to see total revenue, top-selling items, and more.

Troubleshooting

The kiosk is not showing the menu.

This usually means the product list in the POS is empty or has not been saved. Ensure that products are correctly set up in the POS system.

Interface & Features

How to apply a discount?

In the cart details on the right, you can enter a percentage in the "Discount (%)" field. Alternatively, you can enter a valid promo code in the promo code section and click "Apply".

How to enable Dark Mode?

Click the moon/sun icon at the top right of the main content area to toggle between light and dark modes.
